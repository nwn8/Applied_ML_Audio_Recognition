database shema for capstone

Songs
-------
song_id (pk) auto increment
title text
duration num
lyrics text
cleaned_lyrics text
word_count num
unique_word_count num
word_freq object

Fingerprints
-------
fingerprint_id  (pk) Auto Increment
song_id (Fk)
fingerprint (generated by chromaprint) text


Audio_Features  MFCC- a compact numeric summary of what a song sounds like  (Mel-Frequency Cepstral Coefficients)
---------
feature_id (pk) auto increment
song_id (fk)
mfcc_1 generated by Librosa
... 
mvcc_20


When somebody uploads a song 


Upload MP3
      ↓
Extract Fingerprint
      ↓
Extract MFCCs
      ↓
Transcribe Lyrics
      ↓
Compare Against Database


-----------------------------
The fingerprint answers:

"Is this the same recording?"

The MFCCs answer:

"Does this sound similar?"

The lyrics answer:

"Are the words similar?

Create the database... process the song.
Uses Whisper for lyrics
Cleans lyrics
Creates word counts and word frequency
Uses AcoustID/Chromaprint for fingerprinting
Uses Librosa to extract tempo and 20 MFCC features
Inserts everything into SQLite
